eRaksha // YEAR: 2026

01. Protect Your Private Info

When you talk to an AI, think of it like a public forum. Most systems learn from what you tell them.

  • Don't share names: Use "Person A" instead of full real names.
  • Keep IDs private: Never type in phone numbers, home addresses, or ID numbers.
  • Hide work secrets: Do not paste private company documents or internal emails.

02. Double-Check the Facts

AI can sound very confident even when it is wrong. This is often called "hallucinating."

  • Verify with Search: If an AI gives you a date or a law, check it on a trusted website.

03. Use AI for Content, Not Decisions

AI is an assistant, not the boss. You should always make the final call.

04. Watch Out for Scams

New technology makes it easy to fake voices and faces. This is called a "Deepfake."
